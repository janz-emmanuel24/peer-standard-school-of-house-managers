Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our programs and services

How long are the programs?

Our programs range from 2-16 weeks depending on the certification level and specialization you choose.

Do you offer financial aid?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans, scholarships, and employer sponsorship options.

What certifications do you offer?

We offer Basic, Advanced, and Executive certifications in domestic service and household management.

Is job placement guaranteed?

While we can't guarantee employment, we provide comprehensive job placement assistance and have strong industry connections.

Can I study part-time?

Yes, we offer flexible scheduling options including evening and weekend classes.

What are the admission requirements?

You need to be at least 18 years old, have a high school diploma, and pass a background check.
